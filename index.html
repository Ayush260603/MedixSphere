Cardiology

Our cardiology facility offers state-of-the-art care for all heart-related conditions, delivered by a team of highly skilled cardiologists. Utilizing the latest technology and innovative treatments, we provide comprehensive diagnostic services, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing management for heart health. Whether it's preventive care, managing chronic heart conditions, or performing complex procedures, our dedicated team ensures the highest standard of care to support your cardiovascular well-being.

Neurology

Our neurology facility is dedicated to providing exceptional care for a wide range of neurological conditions. Staffed by experienced neurologists and equipped with advanced diagnostic and treatment technologies, we offer comprehensive services for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological disorders. Our personalized treatment plans and compassionate care approach ensure that each patient receives the attention and expertise needed to manage and improve their neurological health. Trust our team to deliver the highest standard of care, supporting you on your journey to optimal neurological well-being.

Hepatology

Our hepatology facility specializes in the comprehensive care of liver-related conditions, providing expert diagnosis and treatment for diseases such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Our team of skilled hepatologists utilizes cutting-edge technology and the latest medical advancements to create personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs. With a commitment to compassionate care and patient education, we strive to improve liver health and enhance the quality of life for those we serve. Trust our dedicated team to deliver exceptional hepatology care for your liver health concerns.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric care facility is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our experienced pediatricians offer a wide range of services, from routine check-ups and immunizations to the management of chronic conditions and acute illnesses. With a child-friendly environment and a focus on holistic care, we ensure that every child receives the attention and medical expertise they deserve for healthy growth and development.

Eye Care

Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the most common queries that are often asked by our patients and clients are here

  • Are your facilities accessible for patients with disabilities?

    Yes, our facilities are fully accessible for patients with disabilities, including wheelchair access and other necessary accommodations.

  • How do I get test results?

    Your Lab test results will be sent either to your registered mail id by which you could have booked an appointment or by SMS Facility on the registered mobile number if you have booked for the lab test in offline mode.

  • Do you offer preventive health services?

    Yes, we offer a range of preventive health services, including vaccinations, health screenings, and wellness check-ups to help maintain your overall health.

  • What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

    Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. You can do this via phone or our online health portal.

  • What services are offered at the Eye Care Centre?

    Our Eye Care Centre provides comprehensive eye examinations, treatments for various eye conditions, and surgical services. You can also read more in the Departments section.
